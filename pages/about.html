Gary Malkasian

A Seattle native, Gary has worked in Software Development at companies such as Microsoft, Puget Sound Energy, Vertafore, and Sogeti. After 26 years in the industry, he decided to teach and call it retirement. He is enrolled in the CWU MAT Program, endorsing in Computer Science and Mathematics to teach at the High School level. He also teaches Computer Science as adjunct faculty at Lake Washington Institute of Technology and Northwest University.

Before his career in software, Gary worked with volunteers and humanitarian aid in Romanian orphanages for six years. He has continued since to be an advocate for children's rights as a foster/adopt dad, often testifying before the state legislature on foster care issues.

Gary is also a fan of Doctor Who and Pokemon Go (Team Instinct).

He lives in Woodinville surrounded by his wife, children, grandbabies, and a couple of cats.
